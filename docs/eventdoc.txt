EVOKE 2K25 - EVENT LIST
========================

Registration Fee: ₹100 per event (pre-register) / ₹150 on-spot
Last Date: 20 Nov 2025
Venue: Lowry Memorial PU College

-------

1. Dumb Charades

8. Instrumental Music

   - Bring your own instruments

9. Face Painting

10. Cooking Without Fire

11. Vegetable Carving

    - 1 hour

12. Pot Painting

13. Art and Craft

14. Collage Competition

15. Pencil Sketch
    - Individual
    - Theme on the spot

16. Best Out of Waste
    - Individual
    - 1½ hours
    - Only waste/recyclable materials; eco-friendly models
