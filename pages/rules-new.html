INTER-SCHOOL FEST

Evoke'2k25 • “Bring out the best in You.”

RULES TO BE FOLLOWED

  • Registration for all the events starts from 8:00 AM to 8:30 AM.
  • Each participant must carry their school ID at all times.
  • Participants must be present for the inaugural program at 8:30 AM.
  • Teams that are late or miss their slot for any event will be disqualified.
  • Partaking in any kind of obscenity will lead to the debarring of the team from any further participation.
  • Illegal or restricted items such as alcohol, tobacco or drugs are strictly prohibited. Weapons, sharp objects or harmful items are not allowed.
  • Participants must strictly follow the code of conduct and maintain discipline.
  • Participants must carry the required equipment for their events.
  • The organizers reserve the right to cancel, reschedule the time or change the venue of any event. A minimum of three teams must be there for an event or it will be canceled.
  • Decision of the judges & coordinators will be final.
  • Tracks for solo dance, group dance, solo instrumental must be submitted in a pendrive at the time of registration.
  • The management will not be responsible for the loss of personal belongings.
  • A participant may register for a maximum of two events.
  • Lunch will be provided for all the Participants.
  • The final event of the day will conclude at 5:00pm.
  • The last date for the FEST registration is 20th November 2025.
  • Participants who register prior for the FEST shall pay a fee of ₹100 per Event.
  • Participants who register on the day of the FEST shall pay a fee of ₹150 per Event.

EVENTS AND RULES

  • 1. Dumb Charades
    • A team must consist of 2 participants.
    • One participant acts out the word or phrase without speaking/sound/no lip-syncing.
    • Another participant must guess the word/phrase enacted.
    • Time limit allotted for guessing is 1 minute; buzzer indicates end of time.
    • Each team will receive 10 words per round; minimum 8 correct to qualify.
    • Judges’ decision is final.
  • 2. G.K. Quiz
    • Prelims written test will be conducted.
    • Two members per team; maximum two teams per school.
    • Quiz includes Aptitude, Current Affairs, Sports, Mathematics, Science, Inventions, Geography.
    • Top 4 qualified teams in prelims participate in final oral round.
    • Judges’ decision will be final.
  • 3. Fashion Show: Theme – Indian Culture (Individual)
    • Outfits must adhere to the theme.
    • Clothing should be appropriate and modest. Sleeveless/below-knee/scandalous/ripped clothes are not allowed.
    • Props, hats, bags, accessories can be utilized.
    • Time limit: 30 seconds on stage.
    • Judges’ decision will be final.
  • 4. Group Song
    • Team of 3 to 5 members.
    • Max 4 minutes; any language; songs must be decent; no religious songs.
    • Judging: Vocal Performance, Musicality, Stage Presence, Technical Skill, Performance Style.
    • Participants can bring their own track/karaoke.
    • Judges’ decision will be final.
  • 5. Solo Song
    • Individual participation; max 2 minutes.
    • Any language; songs must be decent; no religious songs.
    • Own track/karaoke allowed.
    • Judges’ decision will be final.
  • 6. Group Dance
    • Team: 2 to 5 members. Theme – Cinematic.
    • Time limit 3+1 minutes; fusion allowed; no religious acts.
    • Bring track on pen drive; no stage-dirtying particles.
    • Obscene/vulgar music strictly prohibited.
    • Max 2 teams per school.
    • Judges’ decision will be final.
  • 7. Solo Dance
    • Individual; time limit 4 minutes; theme – Free Style; no religious acts.
    • Fusion allowed; bring songs on pen drive.
    • Participants must be decent and perform gracefully.
    • No stage-dirtying particles.
    • Judges’ decision will be final.
  • 8. Instrumental Music
    • Individual; bring your own instruments; time limit 3 minutes.
    • Judges’ decision will be final.
  • 9. Face Painting
    • Team of 2 (one paints on the other’s face).
    • Bring your own materials; time limit 1 hour.
    • Judging: Creativity, Artistic Composition, Originality, Clarity of Theme.
    • Free Theme – draw on any theme.
    • Judges’ decision will be final.
  • 10. Cooking Without Fire
    • Individual; time limit 1½ hours; no fire.
    • Bring own utensils; no ready-to-cook or precooked items.
    • Presentation and creativity judged (including nutrition value).
    • Only snacks, salads, desserts, beverages allowed; display dish name and ingredients.
    • Judges’ decision will be final.
  • 11. Vegetable Carving
    • Individual; 1 hour; follow the given theme.
    • Bring own vegetables, tools, accessories; no fire/electric tools/pre-cut items.
    • Judges’ decision will be final.
  • 12. Pot Painting
    • Pot provided (one per participant).
    • Bring painting materials; no fire or spray paint.
    • Time limit 1 hour; only hand painting; no stickers/decals/readymade decorations.
    • Pot must remain unbroken and cleanly finished.
    • Judges’ decision will be final.
  • 13. Art and Craft
    • Individual; bring own materials/tools; time 1 hour.
    • Original work only; no internet copying; no mobile usage.
    • Judges’ decision will be final.
  • 14. Collage Competition
    • Minimum 2 members per team; bring own materials.
    • Theme announced on the spot; time 1 hour; charts provided.
    • Only handmade collages; no blades/scissors/fire/paints.
    • Judges’ decision will be final.
  • 15. Pencil Sketch
    • Individual; theme on the spot; bring pencils, erasers, sharpeners.
    • Papers provided; only graphite/charcoal pencils.
    • No obscene sketches; originality required.
    • Judges’ decision will be final.
  • 16. Best Out of Waste
    • Individual; time 1½ hours.
    • Bring your own waste/recyclable materials and tools.
    • Prepare on the spot; no pre-prepared items.
    • Item should be useful, creative, eco-friendly; no electric/battery tools.
    • Judges’ decision will be final.
